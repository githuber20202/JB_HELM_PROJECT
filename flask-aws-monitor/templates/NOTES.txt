1. Verify pods and service:
   kubectl get deploy,po,svc -l app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}

2. Access:
   - If ingress.enabled=true: browse the configured host(s).
   - If ingress.enabled=false: fetch external IP:
       kubectl get svc {{ include "flask-aws-monitor.fullname" . }} -o jsonpath='{.status.loadBalancer.ingress[0].ip}'
     Then open: http://EXTERNAL_IP:{{ .Values.service.port }}/
